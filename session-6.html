SESSION DESCRIPTION:
Deep Learning has been gaining the most influence in several research areas of data
analytics, computer vision, big data, cloud -grid computing, remote sensing and other
allied areas. The deep learning has its profound importance in solving the real world
applications. The research areas are not limited to these themes but are even in other
engineering branches like electrical, mechanical, etc. Thus the application of
Computational Intelligence techniques in deep learning and data analytics provides
solutions to various problems during the COVID 19 pandemic situation. This could
include the mask detection along with the identity of vehicle and the person. The Data
Scientists are encouraged to work with the Deep Learning and Data Analytics for the
growth in many tools like the Tensorflow. Lastly but not the least during COVID 19
pandemic there is tremendous increase in Cyber Threats and so Computational
Techniques also play an important role in it.
