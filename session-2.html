SESSION DESCRIPTION: 
This special session on ‘Recent Advances in Smart World Technologies’ aims to provide an effective platform to the academicians, researchers and experts from 
industries across all over the world to discuss the state of research and practice in emerging role of smart technologies such as IoT, Mobile Computing etc. 
for the development of Smart future. Original research papers, practical applications, case studies and state of the art reviews will be accepted. 
It is anticipated that the special session will be opened for new entrance of further research and technology improvements in this important area.

RECOMMENDED TOPICS: 
Smart City
Smart Home
Wearable Technologies
Internet of Things (IoT)
Cloud Computing
Mobile computing
Cognitive Computing
High Performance IoT Computing
Closed and Private Networks
Security, Privacy and Trust in Ubiquitious Computing
